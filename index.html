Pillventory

Count your pills like a pro. Our innovative mobile app is specifically designed for pharmacies, transforming how pharmacists manage medication inventories. By taking a simple photo of pills on their counting tray, the app uses advanced image recognition technology to instantly provide an accurate count. This eliminates the need for meticulous manual counting, significantly enhancing efficiency and accuracy in daily operations. User-friendly and seamlessly integrated into existing workflows, our app ensures a smoother, faster, and error-free pill counting process for pharmacies.
